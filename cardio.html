Cardio Workouts

About Cardio

Cardio, short for cardiovascular exercise, is any type of exercise that raises your heart rate and gets your blood pumping. Common cardio exercises include running, biking, swimming, and jumping rope. Cardio is important for maintaining a healthy heart and lungs, as well as for burning calories and losing weight.

Workouts

We offer a variety of cardio workouts, including:

  • Running
  • Cycling
  • Swimming
  • Jumping rope

Here is a sample workout:

  1. Warm up for 5 minutes by jogging in place or doing jumping jacks
  2. Run at a moderate pace for 20 minutes
  3. Do 1 minute of high-intensity intervals (such as sprinting), followed by 1 minute of rest, for a total of 10 minutes
  4. Cool down for 5 minutes by walking or doing a slow jog
