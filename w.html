Weightlifting Workouts

About Weightlifting

Weightlifting, also known as strength training, is any type of exercise that uses weights or resistance to build strength and muscle mass. Common weightlifting exercises include bench press, squats, deadlifts, and curls. Weightlifting is important for building muscle, increasing bone density, and improving overall health and fitness.

Workouts

We offer a variety of weightlifting workouts, including:

  • Full body workouts
  • Upper body workouts
  • Lower body workouts
  • Core workouts

Here is a sample workout:

  1. Warm up for 5-10 minutes with cardio exercise, such as jogging or cycling
  2. Perform 3 sets of each exercise, with 8-12 reps per set:
    • Bench press
    • Squats
    • Deadlifts
    • Bicep curls
    • Tricep extensions
  3. Cool down with stretching exercises for each muscle group worked
